Position Summary

We are currently seeking an experienced Senior Accounting Technician for our Vancouver office. Under the supervision of a Manager, Senior Manager, Associate, or Partner, the Senior Technician is responsible for completing bookkeeping tasks and file preparation including financial statements and income tax returns for compilation and review engagements.

Involvement with work related to engagement assignments, including review, compilation, and bookkeeping
engagements, in addition to personal and corporate tax returns

compilation and review engagements according to Firm and regulatory standards for clients in a variety of
businesses and non-profit enterprises

coordination with the tax department to ensure queries are cleared and deadlines are met

Associate/Senior Manager/Manager/Partner, as well as keeping them informed of the progress of the
engagement

business numbers, applications for income security programs (CPP,OAS, GIS)
