Job description
Responsibilities

BASIC SUMMARY:
Serve as the lead scientist in the conduct of assigned non-clinical research studies of moderate to high complexity, to include study management, interpretation and reporting of study data, and assuring the regulatory compliance of these projects.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:  
•Function independently as a Project Scientist in the direction and execution of assigned studies in compliance with GLP regulations as they apply to the conduct of non-clinical research.
•Participate in and coordinate all phases of the study planning process with appropriate departments.
•Develop, transfer and validate ligand-binding assays (LBA) for proteins/peptides/biologics concentration determination in biological fluids and tissues using immunoassay platforms such as ELISA and elecro chemiluminescence (ECL).
•Develop and optimize novel LBAs.  Perform advanced literature searches to develop challenging LBA methods employing complex technologies and techniques.
•Transfer validated LBAs from third party laboratories.
•Review assay development results for transfer and validation and study sample analysis.  
•Oversee delegated phase LBA bioanalysis for biological samples in accordance with GLP regulations.
