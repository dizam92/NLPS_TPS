Job description
We are currently seeking a knowledgeable, energetic, and efficient Accounting Manager to join our team!  The Accounting Manager reports to the Director of Operations and will be responsible for managing the company’s accounting functions, including establishing and maintaining accounting principles, practices, and procedures as well as the preparation of financial statements and reporting to internal and external stakeholders as needed.

The Role:
  ·       Provide leadership to the team through departmental improvement, performance management, daily mentorship and skill development.
·       Hands on management of the financial operations including journal entries, accounts payable, accounts receivable and statutory reporting
·       Establish and monitor internal controls to ensure that accounting activities are in accordance with established all regulatory and company policies
