Job description
Reporting to the Manager of Business Solutions, the Product Manager – Business Solutions will be responsible for overseeing a business area portfolio, executing business strategies, product roadmap development, and the coordination of multiple complex projects. The Product Manager – Business Solutions will focus primarily on releasing products for internal users of Trail Appliances.

Duties & Responsibilities
Execute business area strategy and develop product roadmaps that align with the strategy
Build consensus and facilitate dialogue with developers and colleagues
Work with developers to create functional specifications and build mock-ups of proposed solutions
Analyze, understand, document, and improve systems and processes
Communicate regularly with stakeholders and executives on project status, needs, and milestones
