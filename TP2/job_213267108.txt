Location:

1 Presidents Choice Circle, Brampton, Ontario, L6Y 5S5

The decision to join a company is a big one. We have opportunities for hard working, energetic and reliable people just like you.

Why is this role important?

Working with the merchandising and supply chain teams, this is a great entry level position within our Finance team offering future growth opportunities within the company. We look for retail experience, strong analytical skills, flexibility, adaptability and strong communication skills.

What You'll Do:
• Ensure accuracy and timely execution of all requests received for the maintenance of article Master Data

• Responsible for proper execution of the Delist Process ensuring an accurate article

• Ensure appropriate updates in all legacy systems and/or MDM as required
• Coordinate and ensure proactive and timely communication for successful article Master Data, maintenance to business areas.
• Ensure Data Governance policies and procedures are being followed
• Review and analyze daily reports to ensure completeness and data accuracy.
• Adhoc data integrity cleanup projects as required
• Maintain clear communication and positive relationships with both internal and external stakeholders.



• Post Secondary education in Business or related field, or equivalent related experience
• 2 years store level/retail experience is an asset
• Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office applications (Outlook, Word, Excel, Access)
• Familiarity with procurement systems (Product Manager, CICS, VMS).
• Experience/knowledge with SAP is an asset.
• Ability to manage priorities in a demanding and continually changing environment, and to solve problems with high attention to detail.

Core Competencies
• Strong project and time management skills with the ability to prioritize competing projects
• Exceptional interpersonal skills in order to build relationships, and provide sufficient influence to achieve success
• Excellent verbal and written communication skills
• Creative problem solver and strategic thinker with a strong work ethic and ability to work independently in a fast-paced environment



At Loblaw, we seek great people to continually strengthen our culture. We believe great people model our values, are authentic, build trust and make connections.

If that sounds like you, and you are open-minded, responsive to change and up to the challenges provided in a fast-paced retail environment, apply today.
