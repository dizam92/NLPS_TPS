Job description
Reporting to the Manager of Business Solutions, the Software Developer is responsible for the design, development, testing, and support of Trail’s custom business applications. Solving complex problems and improving the end-user’s experience is critical to this role.

Duties & Responsibilities
Design and build product deliverables according to specifications built by the business analyst/project manager and work within a given time frame to complete deliverables
Participate in every aspect of the development and implementation of programs, working with non-technical users and the project manager to ensure full understanding of the change’s implications to current business processes
Provide technical expertise and recommendations in assessing new software projects and initiatives to support and enhance our existing systems
Work with the business analyst/project manager to create technical specifications and build mock-ups of proposed solutions
